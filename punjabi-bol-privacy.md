_Last updated: July 6, 2026_

Punjabi Bol (ਪੰਜਾਬੀ ਬੋਲ) is a Punjabi language–learning game for young
children on Apple TV, iPhone, and Android.

## We collect nothing

Punjabi Bol does **not** collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any
personal information — from children or adults. Specifically, the app has:

- **No internet access.** The app works entirely offline and makes no
  network connections.
- **No accounts, sign-ins, or profiles.**
- **No advertising** and no advertising SDKs.
- **No analytics or tracking** of any kind.
- **No third-party services.**

## What stays on your device

Game progress (stars earned, the last activity played, which sentences
Auto Play has already spoken) and settings (girl/boy/plural voice forms, the
phonetics toggle) are saved only in the app's local storage on your own
device. They never leave it, and deleting the app deletes them.

Speech is generated by your device's built-in text-to-speech voices,
on-device.

## Children's privacy

The app is designed for young children. Because it collects no data at all,
there is no personal information from children (or anyone else) to process
under COPPA, GDPR, or similar laws.

## Changes

If a future update ever changes any of the above, this policy will be
updated first and the change called out in the app's store release notes.
